Maintenance Wash

A detailed exterior wash to thoroughly clean the exterior of the vehicle. Maintanance washes are crucial for keeping your car looking its best.

  • Pre-rinse using pressure washer.
  • All wheels and tyres cleaned with a PH neutral wheel cleaner to remove brake dust and other contamination spots.
  • Citrus pre-wash degreaser applied to all panels to soften dirt.
  • Power rinse loosened dirt.
  • Prewash safe snow foam applied using a foaming lance to safely remove dirt.
  • 2 bucket safe wash using a PH neutral high gloss shampoo and a high quality lambswool mitt for safe and effective cleaning.
  • Safely Hand-dried using a high-quality drying towel.
  • Coat of gloss enhancement applied to give the paint a slick wet look.
  • Tyre gel applied with choice of finishes (Gloss or Satin).
  • Exterior windows and mirrors cleaned.

Approx 4-6 Hours
