About RJM Autocare

Being a car enthusiast, I like to keep my cars clean and looking their best. After purchasing my first car, I was instantly drawn to the prospect of keeping it clean, which has since lead to an interest and some may say "obsession" with detailing.

Starting as a hobby, I have taken this knowledge and experience and formed RJM Autocare. This obsession means every aspect of your car is given the utmost attention to detail ensuring that a high quality is maintained and it drives away looking its best.
